Observation Notes

  • Sound Theory #1 : Despite the train sounds being the loudest, People find car noises and the animal sounds(barking, cats fighting, etc.) to be way more annoying than the sounds of jets and trains.
  • Sound Theory #2 : Regardless of age, I learnt that the longer people have resided in this neighborhood, they have a much easier time tolerating a lot of the noises.
  • Sound Theory #3: People find the noises of cars and animals to be more annoying because they hear them much more than the jets and train. In short sounds of animals and cars are more annoying because they are more frequent.

Conclusions I reached:

In the NOISSEEEEE page, I introducted my hypothesss regarding the noises in my neighborhood and listed out some theories that I had regarding the noises. After conducting my observations and interviews, I have come to the conclusion that my hypothesis was completely reveresed. I had thought that the loudest and most disruptive noise would be the sound of the train horn or the jets but after conducting my research, I found that people find the consistent noises from cars revving down the street or animals barking to be more disruptive to their daily lives. This is because unlike the train or jets, which happen 2 times a week or even less frequently, the sounds from cars and animals happen multiple times a day and can be very hard to ignore and are much more consistent throughout the year.
